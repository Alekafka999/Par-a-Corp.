Roadmap · Roça Hub
PT-BR EN

Four phases to validate, scale, and turn rural data into a network advantage.

A clear view of Roça Hub's evolution: start with a regional MVP, learn from producers and consumers, expand functionality, and activate layers of intelligence, integration, and ecosystem value.

Operating thesis

Validate before building, prioritize direct value for producers, and turn every platform interaction into useful decision-making data.

4 phases 8 MVP deliverables 100+ transactions as the first milestone

Execution plan

From regional validation to a local rural economy ecosystem.

Each phase depends on validation from the previous one, avoiding excessive build before there is real evidence of usage, retention, and perceived value.

01

Phase 1

MVP Minimum Viable Product

1 to 3 months

Validate the business model with a pilot group of producers and consumers in a specific region.

Deliverables

  • Producer and consumer registration
  • Product catalog with photos and prices
  • Basic product search and filtering
  • Cart and checkout with PIX
  • Order management: producer accepts or rejects
  • Basic email notifications
  • Simple administrative dashboard
  • Responsive mobile-first web app

Success criteria

20+active producers
200+registered consumers
100+completed transactions
NPS > 7from producers and consumers
02

Phase 2

Growth

4 to 9 months

Expand functionality based on MVP feedback and scale to new regions.

  • Native mobile app for iOS and Android
  • Chat between producer and consumer
  • Ratings and reputation system
  • Multiple payment methods: card and boleto
  • Delivery scheduling by time window
  • Interactive producer map
  • Public producer profile with gallery
  • Product traceability and origin
03

Phase 3

Intelligence

10 to 14 months

Activate the AI and data layer to generate differentiated value for producers.

  • Insights dashboard for producers
  • Product recommendation engine for consumers
  • Demand forecasting by product and region
  • Intelligent pricing suggestions
  • Opportunity report: high demand, low supply
  • Seasonality analysis
  • Exportable reports for cooperatives and city governments
04

Phase 4

Ecosystem

14 to 18 months

Turn Roça Hub into a complete hub for the local rural economy.

  • Public API for integration with cooperative systems
  • Loyalty program for recurring consumers
  • Input marketplace for suppliers and producers
  • Rural credit module in partnership with fintechs
  • Digital certification for organic or artisanal production
  • Integration with government programs: PAA and PNAE
  • Expansion to other Brazilian states

Principles

What guides product and investment decisions.

The roadmap is directional: it aligns vision, priority, and advancement criteria without freezing the response to the market.

01

Validate before building

Each phase advances only after the previous one is validated through evidence of usage and perceived value.

02

Producer first

Features that generate direct value for producers take priority in scope decisions.

03

Data as product

Every platform interaction should create useful data for operations, market intelligence, and decision-making.

04

Simplicity

The interface must work for producers with low digital familiarity and demanding operational routines.
