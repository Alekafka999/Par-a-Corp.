An 18-month financial model for lean execution and progressive validation.

Our financial model for the first 18 months is based on lean execution, progressive validation, and a transaction-based revenue model.

Core assumptions
18 months initial operating horizon ~7% commission on transactions $10k-$20k estimated capital requirement

Revenue model

Transaction revenue first, additional monetization later.

We generate revenue primarily through a commission on transactions, with additional future revenue streams including premium subscriptions and data insights.

01

Transaction commission

The primary revenue stream is a commission on transactions, estimated at around 7%.

02

Premium subscriptions

Future paid plans can serve producers, B2B buyers, and partner operations once usage is validated.

03

Data insights

Data-driven reports and market intelligence can become a monetizable layer for producers, cooperatives, and partners.

18 months

Financial progression by execution phase.

The financial curve follows product maturity: validation, transaction growth, additional monetization, and a path toward break-even.

Months 1-3

MVP

In the first phase, we expect low revenue and focus on validation, with minimal operational costs.

Months 4-9

Initial scale

We scale transaction volume and onboard producers and B2B buyers, increasing GMV and recurring usage.

Months 10-14

Data and services

We introduce data-driven features and begin monetizing additional services.

Months 15-18

Near break-even

We expect to approach break-even, with growing transaction volume and multiple revenue streams.

Capital requirement

$10,000 to $20,000 for the first 18 months.

Our total capital requirement for the first 18 months is estimated between $10,000 and $20,000, depending on the speed of execution and expansion.

Fallback plan

If adoption is slower, we shift toward B2B-first.

If adoption is slower than expected, we will prioritize a B2B-first model, reduce product scope, and operate with a more managed marketplace approach to ensure early revenue generation.
